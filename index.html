How Artevia works

From browsing to bidding in three steps

Artevia is built for students and emerging creators. Set up your profile, explore curated auctions, and start building your collection.

1. Create an account

Register with your student email to unlock bidding, credits, and your personal profile.

2. Explore auctions

Browse live listings, track bids in real time, and discover work from other students.

3. List your own work

Upload your artwork, set an auction deadline, and invite others to bid on your pieces.
